Internships

Blue Chip AI offers a variety of internship positions in Development, Operations, and Business. Interns handle support tasks and learn through active engagement with projects. Blue Chip AI’s priority is to ensure that the internship program is both professionally and personally rewarding.

Development

Development interns work closely with our team of programmers to implement and improve code while gaining valuable experience in Functional and Object-Oriented programming. In addition to providing support to the core development staff, interns have the opportunity to work on meaningful open-source projects to better the community.

Operations

Operations interns assist in maintaining Blue Chip AI’s servers. The Operations team is the backbone of our company and we are constantly looking for dedicated individuals to help us achieve our mission.

Business

Business interns work alongside our team of dedicated grant writers to acquire grant funding for development projects, generate advertising materials, and create other technical documents.

Interested?

The ideal candidate possesses a strong work ethic and a desire to learn. Programming experience is a plus, but is by no means required.
