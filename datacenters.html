Centralization

Blue Chip AI's data centers strive to be the most cost effective and environmentally friendly data centers in the US. This is achieved through focusing on energy and cost efficiency rather then pure density.

We dramatically reduce energy usage and component failure through centralization of :

  • cooling
  • power
  • networking
  • storage

Cost Effectiveness

Blue Chip AI utilizes modified decomissioned hardware to build clusters at 1/10th to 1/100th the cost of comparable new systems. Reducing the environmental impact of e-waste and up-cycling equipment.

Energy Efficiency

Blue Chip AI prioritises energy efficiency to levels that have not been seen before. While most data centers spend up to 50% of their total energy on cooling, Blue Chip Servers only use 2% of our energy on cooling.
